# Retention Audit — V3 Sprint Baseline

**Date:** 2025-06-23  
**Status:** Post-V2 functional product

---

## Why Would a Child Return?

| Timeframe | Current V2 Drivers | Gaps |
|-----------|-------------------|------|
| **Tomorrow** | Daily habit not built — no login reward loop | No daily reward, no "see you tomorrow" hook |
| **Next week** | Stickers/achievements give collection goals | No personalization, no star economy, no avatar bond |
| **Long-term** | Puzzle packs offer variety | Limited learning payoff, no adaptive challenge |

---

## Habit vs Excitement Features

| Feature | Habit? | Excitement? |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| Sticker collection | Medium — collection loop | High on unlock |
| Achievements | Medium — milestone chase | Medium burst on unlock |
| Puzzle packs | Low — always available | Medium — novelty |
| Victory confetti | Low — per session | High — momentary |
| Parent dashboard | None for child | None |
| Ghost/hints | Low — utility | Low |

**Missing habit drivers:** daily reward, returning player greeting, avatar identity, star progression.

**Missing excitement drivers:** sound celebration, fun facts, accessory unlocks, difficulty coach praise.
